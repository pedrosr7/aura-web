How do I cancel my subscription?

Manage it in your App Store (Settings › [your name] › Subscriptions) or Google Play (Play Store › Payments & subscriptions). Cancelling stops the next renewal; access lasts until the period ends.

How do I delete my account?

In Aura, open Profile › Account › Delete account. Your account locks immediately and is permanently deleted after 30 days. Details: account deletion.

I deleted my account by mistake

Log back in within 30 days and it's restored.

How do I restore my purchases?

On Aura's subscription screen, tap Restore purchases (handy after reinstalling or switching device).
